Mission Pastor Martti Falck and his team have been doing missionary and aid work in Haiti since 2002. Working directly with trusted partners in Haiti, ensures our aid reaches those in need. Our partners in Haiti are local churches, parishioners and other aid organisations. Over the past 8 years we have organized many aid and evangelical missions in the areas around Port-au-Prince, Jacmel and Jean Rabel. Through these campaigns we have reached tens of thousands of Haitians in need.
